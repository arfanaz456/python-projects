# Restaurant Order System

## Overview
This Restaurant Order System is a simple Python application that allows customers to place orders from a menu and calculates the total bill. The menu includes a variety of items such as pizza, biryani, fries, and more.

## Features
- **Menu Display:** Shows the available items and their prices.
- **Order Placement:** Allows customers to order items from the menu.
- **Order Validation:** Checks if the ordered items are available in the menu.
- **Total Calculation:** Calculates the total amount for the ordered items.

## Menu
- Pizza: Rs200
- Biryani: Rs150
- Pulao: Rs120
- Fries: Rs100
